About ig-api

ig-api is a transparent and secure platform that allows you to compare your followers and following lists on Instagram without the need for providing your Instagram account credentials. We believe in empowering users to have full control over their data.

With ig-api, you can easily download your data from Instagram in a secure manner, and then upload that data to our platform for comparison. We provide a hassle-free experience without compromising your privacy.

We prioritize transparency and ensure that your data is handled securely. We do not require your Instagram login credentials, and we don't store or transmit any sensitive information. Your data is processed locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Our mission is to provide you with valuable insights into your Instagram followers and following lists, allowing you to understand your network better and make informed decisions.
