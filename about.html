About Insightful

Insightful is a transparent and secure platform that allows you to gather analytics on their public or private Instagram accounts in a safe and secure manner. You can now compare your followers and following lists on Instagram and so much more without the need for providing your Instagram username or password. We believe in empowering users to have full control over their data.

With Insightful, you can seamlessly either download or view your analytics from Instagram in a secure manner, by simply uploading your data to our platform for analysis. We provide a hassle-free experience without compromising your privacy.

We prioritize transparency and ensure that your data is handled securely. We do not require your Instagram login credentials, and we don't store or transmit any sensitive information. Your data is processed locally on your device, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Our mission is to provide users with valuable insights into their Instagram audience allowing you to understand your network better and make informed decisions.
