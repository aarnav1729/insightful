How to Download Your Data from Instagram

Follow these steps to download your data from Instagram:

  1. Go to the Instagram website and log in to your account.
  2. Click on your profile picture at the top right corner of the screen.
  3. Select "Settings" from the drop-down menu.
  4. Scroll down to the "Privacy and Security" section and click on "Data Download".
  5. Click on "Request Download" and enter your email address.
  6. Instagram will send you an email with a link to download your data.
  7. Open the email and click on the download link.
  8. Save the downloaded file to your computer.

Once you have downloaded your data from Instagram, you can come back to our website and upload the downloaded file to compare your followers and following lists.
