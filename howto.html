How to Download Your Data from Instagram

  1. Navigate to your profile using the icon with your profile picture on the bottom right corner
  2. Once you are on your profile, click on the hamburger menu on the right corner
  3. Select "Your Activity" from the drop-down menu
  4. Scroll all the way to the bottom down where you will see "Download your Information"
  5. Click on "Request Download" and choose which account/s you want to download data from then click on "Next"
  6. Then choose the option to "Select Types of Information" and select as much or as little information from your account that you want to view analytics on
  7. Once you are done you can select the data range for your data (last week, last month, last 3 months, last 6 months, last year and all time)
  8. Then hit Submit Request and based on the amount of information you've requested Instagram will email you a copy of your data available for download!
  9. Instagram will send you an email with a link to download your data
  10. Upload your data on insightful and view detailed analytics almost instantaneously.
